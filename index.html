Phase 1: Craniofacial Mechanics

The Golden Rule

Tongue should be suctioned to the roof of the mouth, not just touching it. This supports the maxilla and prevents facial sagging over time.

Nasal Dominance

Mouth breathing leads to a recessed jaw and "long face" syndrome. Tape your mouth at night if you are a habitual mouth breather.

Phase 2: Silhouette Architecture (The V-Taper)

To achieve the ideal Golden Ratio (1.618 shoulder-to-waist), you must prioritize specific muscle groups that contribute to width without adding bulk to the waist.
